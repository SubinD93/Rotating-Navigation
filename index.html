Ideal Prime Beach Hotel

Turkey, Marmaris

One of the best hotels in Marmaris, Ideal Prime Beach Hotel is located on the seashore, next to its own sandy and pebbly beach, 110 km from Dalaman airport. It offers several swimming pools, water slides, restaurants and bars, as well as a night club. Most rooms have sea or mountain views. The hotel is suitable for a romantic and peaceful family vacation.

Our hotel

UAI - Ultra all inclusive 24 hours The concept includes: breakfast, late breakfast/brunch, lunch, dinner - buffet, drinks are served by kitchen staff in all bars, night snack; snacks, pastries and desserts - at certain hours, the pub, the nightclub is temporarily closed (due to the pandemic), local and some imported alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. A la carte restaurants - with a stay of 7 days or more, one free visit to one of the restaurants upon reservation. Some soft drinks, tea and coffee are available 24 hours a day in the lobby bar. The hotel does not provide packed lunches.
